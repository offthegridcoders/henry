Castle in the Clouds

Half Marathon & 5k Trail Race

Sunday November 1st, 2015

One of the most beautiful and challenging half marathon courses in New Hampshire!

The post race party is one not to miss. For one it is inside and I offer a free beer with registration and the post race food is incredible! Whoopie pies, hot-cider doughnuts, soup, and more!

USATF Certified Half Marathon Course

  • Start at the Castle in the Cloud, and continue down the Castle Auto Road
  • Left onto Route 171
  • Right onto Sodom Road
  • Right onto County Road (partial dirt road)
  • Left to make a loop out to 109, then back to County Road
  • Continue onto 109
  • Right onto Severance Road
  • Left onto Route 171
  • Right, back onto the Castle Auto Road (all uphill!)
  • Finish just past the Castle Carriage House near the field at the Lakes Region Conservation Trust Trail system
  • * There will be aid stations at miles 3, 6, 8, 10, and 12, with water and electrolyte drinks *

Proceeds from the Race Benefit

  • Help us reach our PKD donation goal of $2,500 before the race!

  • Lakes Region Food Pantry

    The food pantry will be giving out healthy food vouchers for families in need.
